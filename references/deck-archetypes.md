# Deck archetypes

## Strategy consulting

1. Executive summary
2. Context and market change
3. Core problem / opportunity
4. Internal diagnosis
5. Strategic options
6. Recommended strategy
7. Financial / operating impact
8. Implementation roadmap
9. Risks and mitigations
10. Decisions required

## IT / cloud transformation

1. Executive summary
2. Current-state pain points
3. Business and technology objectives
4. Application / data / infrastructure assessment
5. Target architecture
6. Migration or modernization waves
7. TCO / business case
8. Governance and operating model
9. Risks and controls
10. Next steps

## Sales proposal / pre-sales deck

1. Client challenges
2. Business value hypothesis
3. Proposed solution
4. Architecture and delivery model
5. Differentiators
6. Implementation roadmap
7. Investment and ROI
8. Risk management
9. Commercial next steps

## Investment / equity analysis

1. Investment summary
2. Company overview
3. Industry context
4. Financial performance
5. Capital allocation / dividend / buyback
6. Valuation
7. Risks
8. Investment conclusion

## Vendor / option evaluation

1. Decision context
2. Evaluation criteria
3. Options considered
4. Scoring and evidence
5. Risk / dependency analysis
6. Recommended option
7. Implementation plan

## Executive decision meeting

1. Decision required
2. Situation and complication
3. Options
4. Recommendation
5. Financial / operating impact
6. Risks and mitigations
7. Required approval

## Workshop material

1. Objectives
2. Context primer
3. Discussion questions
4. Breakout structure
5. Templates / exercises
6. Synthesis page
7. Follow-up actions
