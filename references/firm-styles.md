# Firm-style notes

These are directional style cues, not brand templates.

## McKinsey-like

- Very strong action titles and pyramid logic.
- Minimal color, high information density, clear source lines.
- Works best for executive decision decks.

## BCG-like

- More conceptual framing and market-structure exhibits.
- Strong use of 2x2, portfolio maps and future-state narratives.
- Works best for strategy and transformation decks.

## Bain-like

- Direct, practical, recommendation-forward pages.
- Emphasis on value creation, customer economics and implementation.
- Works best for commercial strategy and operating improvement.

## Accenture / Deloitte-like

- More suitable for technology transformation and implementation.
- Strong architecture, roadmap, governance, capability maturity and operating-model pages.
- Works best for cloud, AI, data and security consulting decks.
